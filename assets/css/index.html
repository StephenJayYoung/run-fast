About


As an avid athlete in high school, I started doing yoga to help counterbalance the intense stress being put on my body, as well as to build strength in a way that no other sport had ever offered me before. After getting bored with the monotony of Bikram yoga, I started to branch out with other styles and discovered a whole different side to yoga that I hadn't ever known existed. I began to understand that yoga goes way beyond the physical workout, and dives deep into many other aspects of life through so many different limbs. Overwhelmed, yet empowered, I wanted to share yoga as a tool to heal and empower, just like I had experienced in my own practice.

In 2013, immediately after graduating high school in Lynnwood, Washington, I stumbled upon YoYoYogi's Power Yoga Teacher Training and I went for it! I moved to Portland and received my 200-RYT training that November, and had the privilege of learning from amazing teachers like and Alex and Terri Cole.

If you decide to come to my classes, you will find that they are very lighthearted and fun with funky music, and usually involve a fusion of vinyasa and yin yoga with an emphasis on alignment. I also love to supplement with easy meditation practices, as well as adjustments.

Monday


Power Vinyasa Flow

Time: 5:45 pm - 7 pm
Studio: Yoga Refuge
Address: 7831 Southeast Stark Street, Portland, OR 97215

This class is designed to be a fast-paced and dynamic flow. I love to start off slowly, and then gradually build up the intensity to warm up the body and mind for a juicy practice. I enjoy playing with many different standing balance variations, as well as twists, and hip openers. The emphasis of each class is to allow the breath to guide each posture into the next-- breathing through the intensity and finding ease. There are always a few yin postures near the end of my classes, and oh boy will your body be ready for some deep stretches by then!

Tuesday


Yoga For Cyclists

Time: 7 pm - 8 pm
Studio: Vibrant Studios
Address: 1532 SW Jefferson St, Portland, OR 97201

Come roll out your mat, and join a vibrant community of other endurance athletes and cyclists for some yoga! Whether you're a triathlete, once a week jogger, bicycle commuter, or professional athlete, this class will be able to serve whatever type of moving you do. "Yoga for Cyclists" focuses on strengthening the core to improve posture, as well as full body dynamic and static stretching for all-around improved flexibility. My goal is to help prevent and heal sports related injuries, pay attention to muscles that might get neglected to promote full-body strength, and to provide stress management tools with breathing exercises. So come loosen those tight hips and hamstrings, and join me for class!
