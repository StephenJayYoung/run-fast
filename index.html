Background

Background

A Native Oregonian, Jen was born and raised in West Linn, where she attended West Linn High School and ran on the cross country team. She has had a lifelong passion and love of running since her childhood, and is now the physical therapist for West Linn High School's cross country team, working closely alongside head coach Jay Shrotzberger, where she develops and implements their running injury prevention and core strengthening programs. Jen also presents injury prevention and running form lectures for Foot Traffic's Women's Academy and Foot Traffic University running programs.

Education

Education

After graduating from West Linn High School, Jen attended the University of Boulder, where she earned her BA in Kinesiology and applied Physiology. She then went on to receive her Doctorate in Physical Therapy from Regis University in Colorado, where she was trained by some of the leading clinical practicioners and researchers in the field of Orthopedic and Manual Physical Therapy. She obtained extensive training in spinal and peripheral joint thrust manipulation and orthopedics.

Jen is an active member of The American Physical Therapy Associates (APTA), and orthopedic section member, member of the American Academy of Orthopedic and Manual Physical Therapy (AAOMPT), and member of Kinetacore.

Interests

Interests

In her spare time, Jen enjoys running, rock climbing, paddle boarding, singing, traveling and spending time with her family and friends
